Sauerkraut recipe from memory

Please check other sources before using :)

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Kosher Salt
  • Juniper Berries

Recipe

  1. Finely slice the cabbage and place in non-reactive bowl.
  2. Mix with more salt than you think you need.
  3. You probably didn't add enough salt. Add more salt & mix.
  4. Add juniper berries & mix. There's really no right or wrong amount here.
  5. Let sit for an hour or two.
  6. Put mixture in big jar or crock, tamping early and often.
  7. Make sure all solids are under brine - I love the plastic-bag-filled-with-water method of weighing things down
  8. Place in a dark place on a tray in case it overflows while bubbling.
  9. Depending on its progress and your desired level of fermentation, move to fridge in anywhere from two days to two weeks.
  10. Enjoy!
